# Bill Mahon Photo

> Professional Photography, Video, and Drone Photography in Sacramento, California for Corporate and Business Advertising and Marketing Needs.

Bill Mahon is a commercial photographer, videographer, and FAA Part 107 certified drone pilot based in Sacramento, CA. With over 35 years of professional experience and operating since 1992, Bill Mahon Photo serves businesses and corporations throughout Northern California and travels nationwide. The studio is rooted in portrait photography and has since specialized in corporate photography, commercial advertising, drone aerial work, and professional video production.
