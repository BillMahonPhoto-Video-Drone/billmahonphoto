# Bill Mahon Photo

> Professional Photography, Video, and Drone Photography in Sacramento, California for Corporate and Business Advertising and Marketing Needs.

Bill Mahon is a commercial photographer and videographer based in Sacramento, CA since 1992, with over 35 years of professional experience. He is a FAA Part 107 certified drone pilot. His work spans corporate photography, drone photography, commercial advertising, and professional video. He serves clients in Northern California and travels nationwide.

## Services

- **Drone Photography**: Aerial photography and video for commercial and advertising use
- **Corporate Photography**: Corporate group photos, business headshots, portraits, and events
- **Commercial Photography**: People, products, and architecture for marketing needs
- **Professional Video**: Corporate video production with audio and multimedia capabilities
- **Drone Videos**: Aerial video for commercial and advertising purposes
- **Stock Photography**: Commercial stock images available
- **On-Location & Studio**: Available for both on-location and studio shoots
